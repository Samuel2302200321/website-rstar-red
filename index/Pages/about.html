Short history of the parent company.

Rockstar was founded in December of 1998 by, Sam and Dan Houser, Tery Donovan, Jamie King, and Gary J. Foreman. Rockstar published Their first product, that being Grand Theft Auto on April 6th, 1999. they sold over 3 milllion copies world wide. The game earned the honor of PlayStation's greatest hits.

Rockstar Red.

Rockstar Red is a sub branch similar to other sub branches like, Rockstar North based in Scotland, We are based in the caribbean. Red Will be the lead developers when it comes to game remasters and ports, to modern hardware. These game ports will be released both digitally and physically. Our goal at Red is mainly Game preservation.

Partners

Rockstar Red plans to partner with a lot of game publishers, to also bring their products to modern hardware as well. We hope through this, Rockstar Red can help publishers and game developers establish a better relationship with our parent company Rockstar.

Our secondary goal is also to partner with any publisher or developers that also has old classics, so we can also port them to modern hardware.
